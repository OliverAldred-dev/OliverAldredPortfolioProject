Who I Am

I'm a passionate developer and designer with a love for creating beautiful, functional digital experiences. With experience in game development and 3D modeling, Im currently on my way to completing my college course on game development to achieve a RSL level 3 in Art and Design. In this course I have been able to learn a lot about the different aspects of game development and have been able to create a variety of projects that showcase my skills in design, development, and problem-solving. During the years of my college course I have participated in multiple game jams and learnt about the importance of teamwork in the workspace and how to work with others to create engaging games.

My journey in technology as a whole started with a curiosity about how games work and websites online. Since then, I've become very much interested in the process of creating games and websites, and have been dedicated to learning the skills necessary to bring my ideas to life. I have experience working in programming languages like C#, python, and have used CSS, Html and java through the making of this website.

When I'm not coding or building levels for games, you can find me exploring new ways of creation in the digital space, through making more games experimenting with other types of code or exploring cyber security online.
